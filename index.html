About Diaspora Yatra

Diaspora Yatra is a campaign to promote diaspora and privacy in digital communications.

We reach out to schools, colleges and organizations and engage with them on the need of protecting privacy in digital communications.

We use videos, talks, workshops and debates to convey our ideas.

  • Who keeps the data we share on services like Facebook and Whatsapp?
  • How can they provide a free service for everyone?
  • Are they charity services?
  • How do they pay for their servers, bandwidth, and employees?
  • Are ads in them similar to how newspapers and TV channels show advertisements?
  • Can we have a better deal?
  • What is diaspora*?
  • How is diaspora* different?
  • How can we start using diaspora?
  • How can we help diaspora?
